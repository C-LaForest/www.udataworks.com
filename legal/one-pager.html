Sovereign AI is the headline differentiator. I help firms adopt AI on infrastructure they control — keeping privileged communications, work product, and matter data inside the firm rather than handing them to a third-party AI vendor. Nothing leaves your walls, so there is nothing for an opposing party to subpoena from a vendor and no outside disclosure to argue waiver from.
With 25+ years leading technology in mission-critical, confidentiality-driven environments, I help managing partners, general counsel, and legal-ops leaders bring AI capability into the firm without creating a new external holder of client confidences. My approach is vendor-neutral, confidentiality-first, and grounded in operational reality.
