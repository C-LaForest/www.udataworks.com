Headline Service

Sovereign AI Strategy & Implementation

Adopt AI without creating a new external holder of your clients' confidences.

Sovereign AI is the differentiated capability I bring to legal practices. Two distinct offerings under one umbrella: strategy work that gets you a defensible plan, and implementation work that turns the plan into a working AI capability your attorneys can use — entirely on infrastructure the firm controls.

Sovereign AI Strategy

  • Audit where AI already touches privileged material — shadow use, vendor dependencies, data exposure
  • Map exposure against confidentiality, privilege, work-product, and outside-counsel-guideline considerations
  • Build/buy/host recommendation aligned to your firm's obligations, not the vendor's terms
  • Vendor neutrality assessment — open-weight in-house models vs licensed vs SaaS, with clear trade-offs
  • Briefing for partners, GC, or the risk committee on options, costs, and timeline
  • Governance that ties AI use into your existing conflicts and records programs, not a new silo

Sovereign AI Implementation

  • On-premises or firm-controlled private-cloud AI infrastructure design and deployment
  • Open-weight model selection, in-house hosting, and evaluation against your real workflows
  • AI governance program: model registry, versioning, prompt/response logging, human-in-the-loop review
  • Audit trail kept inside the firm — a record you hold, not a vendor's to produce
  • Integration with document management, matter management, and identity/ethical-wall systems
  • Hands-on coordination across IT, security, records, and practice leadership
Most legal AI initiatives stall at one question: "where does the client's data go?" Sovereign AI answers it by keeping the data inside the firm — so there is no vendor to subpoena and no outside disclosure to build a waiver argument on.
Scope note: These engagements deliver technology strategy and architecture, not legal advice. I help you build AI that keeps privileged material in-house; the privilege, ethics, and discovery judgments about your matters remain with you and your counsel.

Reference Implementation

Rather than point to law-firm client names, I point to working software. The sovereign-AI patterns I recommend are the ones I have already built and run.

Privileged-Inbox Triage (Email Triage)

A privacy-first email automation system that classifies, prioritizes, routes, and drafts replies — running entirely on infrastructure the operator controls. The default model is local; email never leaves the network. It is a working reference implementation of sovereign AI at the application layer: exactly the pattern a firm would use to let AI touch a privileged inbox without a vendor ever holding the messages.

Sovereign-by-design controls: local-by-default LLM, a hard-locked local-only embedding allowlist, and a full in-house audit trail. Any cloud option is opt-in and gated — not the default. HIPAA-style handling is aligned to, not certified against, published standards.
