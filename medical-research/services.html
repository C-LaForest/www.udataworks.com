Sovereign AI Strategy & Implementation for Research

Adopt AI in your research program without compromising IRB protocols, grant terms, or publication standing.

Sovereign AI is the differentiated capability I bring to medical research programs. Two distinct service offerings: strategy work that gets you a defensible plan for your IRB and grants office, and implementation work that turns the plan into operational research AI capability.

Sovereign AI Strategy for Research Programs

  • Audit current AI footprint in your program — shadow usage, data exposure, vendor dependencies
  • Map regulatory exposure: HIPAA, IRB protocols, FDA 21 CFR Part 11, grant terms, DUAs, NIH data policies
  • Build/buy/host architecture recommendation for your research workloads
  • Vendor neutrality assessment — open-weight biomedical models vs licensed vs commercial
  • IRB and grants-team-ready briefing document
  • Integration with existing research compliance program (no new silo)

Sovereign AI Implementation for Research

  • Sovereign infrastructure design — institutional HPC, research enclaves, hybrid patterns
  • Open-weight model selection, biomedical fine-tuning, evaluation against research benchmarks
  • Federated learning architecture for multi-site cohorts and rare disease consortia
  • AI governance program — model registry, lineage tracking, IRB-aware audit trails, FDA Part 11 compliance
  • Reproducibility infrastructure — model versioning tied to published results
  • Hands-on coordination across PI, IRB, IT, security, grants office, data team
Most research AI initiatives stall at one of two questions: "is this in our IRB protocol?" or "will this hold up at publication?" Sovereign AI answers both — by keeping data inside your institution's perimeter and giving you the audit trail and model lineage that journals, funders, and regulators are starting to require.
